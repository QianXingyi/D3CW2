This page is for the user who want to know the approximate distribution of different countries or power stations.
Each dot on the map represents a power station.
The table below is the total number of power stations and total power generation in each country.
Put the mouse on the dot in the map to see the name and power generation capacity of the corresponding power station.
The map can also be simply zoomed in and out with the mouse wheel.
The components of this page is from bootstrap.
All visual rendering and dynamic effects are achieved through d3.js.
